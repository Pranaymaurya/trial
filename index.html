WELCOME TO MG'S ROASTARY




WELCOME YOU ALL WE MG'S ROASTARY ARE WERE DELIGHTED TO HAVE YOU HERE WITH US WE GROW AND IMPORT WORLD'S MOST AUTHENTIC & DELICIOUS COFFEE AND SERVE IT TO YOU THE COFFEE PRODUCTION PROCESS STARTS WITH HARVESTING CHERRIES FROM THE MATURE COFFEE PLANTS. THESE CHERRIES CONTAIN SEEDS, WHICH ARE THE COFFEE BEANS. THE DRY METHOD, WET METHOD, AND SEMI-DRY METHOD ARE THE THREE MAJOR COFFEE PROCESSING METHODS. AFTER THE COFFEE FRUIT IS PROCESSED, THE COFFEE BEANS ARE RUN THROUGH THE MILLING PROCESS, WHICH INCLUDES HULLING, POLISHING, CLEANING, GRADING, AND SORTING. THE COFFEE BEANS ARE ROASTED AND GROUND TO PRODUCE GROUND COFFEE, WHICH IS BREWED TO MAKE THAT CUP OF COFFEE.
